Welcome to our final project page!

The current work provides statistical measures about soccer World Cup and applies the computer graphics concepts that were learned throughout the course to implement statistical graphs and non-traditional data representation bringing data to life. Spanning for over 84 years and 20 tournaments, the World Cup provides a wealth of statistical data that can be used with a variety of visualization techniques.

Introduction

The World Cup is the one of the biggest international sporting events (second only to the Summer Olympic Games). The event is held every four years where currently 32 nations compete for the biggest soccer's prize. The tournament started in 1930 with 13 nations and the last tournament was held in 2014. The 1942 and 1946 tournaments were cancelled as a result of World War II. There were a total of 20 tournaments. The next event will be held in Russia in 2018.
